\# Centralized E-Commerce Platform for Laptop Sales \& Auction Management  

\### Group 8 ‚Äì Project README



\## üìå Overview  

This project is a \*\*centralized web-based e-commerce platform\*\* designed to streamline the buying, selling, and auctioning of laptops and related computer peripherals. The system brings together \*\*customers\*\*, \*\*sellers\*\*, and an \*\*admin team\*\* into a single unified platform where users can browse products, compare options, place orders, and participate in auctions.



The platform supports multiple vendors, enabling both individuals and organizations to sell their products while ensuring customers have access to a wide variety of laptops and accessories. In addition to standard e-commerce features, an \*\*integrated auction system\*\* allows sellers to list special or limited-edition items for bidding.



This repository will serve as the workspace for documentation and future development.



---



\## üéØ Motivation  

Finding reliable laptops and trusted vendors can be challenging for customers, while sellers often struggle with visibility and streamlined sales processes. This platform aims to:



\- Reduce customer effort in finding quality laptops  

\- Provide sellers with a simple and automated way to manage inventory and listings  

\- Offer a transparent and fair auction system  

\- Lower entry barriers for small vendors to participate in e-commerce  

\- Ensure smooth, error-free handling of orders, payments, and customer data  



Overall, the system connects buyers and sellers efficiently while maintaining fairness, accuracy, and convenience.

\## ‚ú® Main Features  



\### \*\*1. User Authentication \& Role-Based Access\*\*  

\- Supports login through email and third-party providers  

\- Roles include:  

&nbsp; - \*\*Customer\*\* ‚Äì browse, compare, bid, and purchase  

&nbsp; - \*\*Seller\*\* ‚Äì manage product listings and view customer details  

&nbsp; - \*\*Admin\*\* ‚Äì moderate users and oversee system operations  



\### \*\*2. Product Listing \& Inventory Management\*\*  

\- Sellers can add, update, or remove products  

\- Inventory updates automatically with each purchase  



\### \*\*3. Product Browsing \& Comparison\*\*  

\- Users can view available products, compare specifications, and check reviews  

\- Post-purchase reviews and ratings are supported  



\### \*\*4. Auction System\*\*  

\- Sellers can create auction listings  

\- Buyers can place bids during the auction period  

\- System automatically selects and notifies the winning bidder  



\### \*\*5. Order Placement \& Payment Handling\*\*  

\- Customers can place orders or win auctions  

\- Multiple payment options supported (e.g., bank transfer, cash on delivery)  

\- Sellers receive order notifications  

\- System logs all order activity for future reference
